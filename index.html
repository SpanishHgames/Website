Welcome to SpanishHgames!

We are dedicated to translating 18+ visual novel games into Spanish for the Spanish-speaking audience. Our passion lies in making these gaming experiences accessible and enjoyable for Spanish-speaking players. We strive to provide accurate and high-quality translations, ensuring that the essence and emotion of the original game are preserved in our Spanish version. We take pride in offering Spanish-speaking gamers the opportunity to immerse themselves in exciting and captivating stories, providing them with a complete and engaging gaming experience.

About Us

We are a small organization dedicated to the translation of games classified as adults. Our organization is made up of 2 people at the moment. Our goal is to provide accurate and high quality translations, ensuring that players can enjoy these games in their native language. Despite being small, we are committed to providing excellent translations and improving the gaming experience for our audience.
